Why my completed offers not showing on withdrawal history?

If you don't see your offer on withdrawal history that you completed, that means your offer still did't confirm by Offerwall provider or by advertisers. we don't have any control of it.

Why my completed offer are holding?

Some offerwall tasks credited to you account after certain amount of time. You can check your account history offerwalls tabs to see when your payment will be sent to you. The advertisers need to verify the authencity of data before they mark as completed.

Why my completed offer status is reversed and my balance is negative?
